The Problem

Fresh graduates have years of real skills built through projects, events, and competitions — but no honest way to surface them. CVs are written from memory. Employers can't verify claims. Real talent stays invisible.

The Solution

Worthy is a living portfolio platform where every post a candidate makes is read by an AI engine that extracts skills, links them to evidence, and updates the portfolio automatically. No CV. Just proof.

How It Works — Three Engines
01
Extract & Question
Candidate posts a real experience. AI extracts skills, evidence quotes, evidence strength, trending tags, and an employer quick-scan — all in one pipeline call. Live clarifying questions appear as they type.
02
Position & Connect
Ask Worthy analyses the full portfolio against market trends and returns a positioning summary, 2–3 realistic career pathways, and matched mentor suggestions with connection requests.
03
Trend Digest
A periodic digest showing which skills are rising, stable, or declining in the job market. Keeps the platform useful between job searches and feeds a future university curriculum engine.
